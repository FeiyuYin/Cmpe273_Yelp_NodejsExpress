    Awesome authentic Jamaican food.  I had the oxtails just like I always do (I am a regular at their Menlo Park location) and it was just as good.  Loved the jerk chicken, and yes it is spicy so just stop trippin',  if you want your food bland go somewhere else and don't mess it up for the rest of us.

    They have housemade ginger beer so skip the bottle. Good stuff here. Don't miss it!

  • Huy D.

    Some people say you can't believe, Jamaica we have a bobsled team.  We have the one Derice, the one Junior, Yule Brynner and the man Sanka - the fastest of the fastest of Jamaica sprinters. Respect to the man Irv Blitzer!  Feel the rhythm.  Feel the rhyme.  Get on up - it's bobsled time!

    God, I love Cool Runnings.  Next to the Mighty Ducks, it's one of my favorite sports movie.

    One day, I'll be able to travel to Jamaica and run alongside the beaches with Usain Bolt.  But for time being, I'll read more » settle for Back a Yard's amazing jerk chicken and ox tail.  If you can deal with the wait time, you'll be rewarded with the most tender and flavorful pieces of cooked protein in San Jose.  Rich with herbs and spices, the jerk chicken and ox tail have been infused with the spirit of Jamaica in every bite.  Always go for the combo so you can get the best of both worlds.  And the spiciness?  It's legitimate.  Subtle heat that builds with every piece you eat.  

    The place is always packed during lunch hours for good reason.  With that said, you should allot at least 30-40 minutes.  It takes that long to get your food.  But relax mon.  You're in good hands.  Enjoy the chill island vibes before getting your grub on. « show less

    I've been getting jerk chicken and ox tail all this time, thinking they were the best items on the menu. could be better than that. But I finally tried their ribs..

    Wow!

    They're juicy and tender, have the perfect amount of flavoring without being overly sauced. They're different than American style BBQ ribs, and I actually like these way more! I absolutely love their plantains, rice and salad too. They're so simple but delicious.

    So next time, I'll starve myself and get jerk chicken, ox tail read more » AND ribs on the side. And a beer. Because apparently they have pretty good happy hour every Mon-Fri from 5-6. « show less
